Introduction

We have developed an innovative animation learning platform that encompasses a comprehensive range of animation courses. Our platform goes beyond traditional methods by incorporating advanced technologies to enhance the learning experience.


One of the key features of our platform is its ability to assess students' knowledge through assignments. Each course includes a series of assignments designed to evaluate the students' understanding and application of the animation concepts taught. We have integrated a machine learning model into our platform to automatically grade these assignments. This ensures objectivity and efficiency in evaluating student performance.


Additionally, our platform offers an animation creation tool, which allows users to create simple 2D animations. This tool serves as a valuable resource for studying and practicing the principles of animation. By actively engaging with the creation process, users gain a deeper understanding of animation techniques and can apply them in their own projects.

  • Main Objective

    The principal aim of this research project is to foster knowledge sharing and promote skill development in the field of animation among Sri Lankan students. We intend to achieve this by creating an interactive, user-friendly web-based application that serves as a comprehensive learning platform.

  • Specific Objectives

    1. Providing an opportunity to interact with leading animation designers and students.

    2. Evaluating students’ activities and testing their knowledge.

    3. Supplying essential assets.

    4. Introduce new animators to the job market.

    5. Using Machine Learning for assessment grading.

    6. Knowledge sharing whole animation processes such as 3d animation, 2d animation, broadcasting animation, lighting, rendering, texturing, and modern technology that come across the animation industry.

    • Research Methodology


    • 01

      Pre and post-assessments

      Conduct assessments to measure the learning outcomes and progress of students before and after utilizing the platform. Compare the results of students who actively use the animation creation tool and those who do not.

    • 02

      Assignment grading comparison

      Compare the grades assigned by the machine learning model with the grades given by human evaluators for the same assignments. Analyze the level of agreement and identify any discrepancies.

    • 03

      Surveys and interviews

      Administer surveys or conduct interviews to gather feedback from students regarding their experiences with the automated assignment grading system and animation creation tool. Explore their perceptions of the effectiveness, usability, and impact on their learning journey.

    • 04

      Usage analytics

      Collect data on the usage patterns of the animation creation tool. Analyze the frequency and duration of tool usage to determine its impact on students' engagement and practice of animation principles.


    User Guidance


    • Our Learning Management System (LMS) is a cutting-edge platform designed to revolutionize the way you learn. With a user-friendly interface and robust features, our LMS is your gateway to a world of knowledge and skill development.


      Sign up: Visit our website and create an account by providing your details, such as name, email address, and password. Alternatively, you can log in using your existing social media accounts.

    • Explore the Course Catalog: Once you've signed in, browse through our comprehensive course catalog. You'll find a wide range of animation courses covering various topics, techniques, and skill levels. Take your time to explore the available options and choose a course that suits your interests and skill level.


      Enroll in a Course: After selecting a course, click on the "Enroll" or "Start Learning" button to enroll in the course. Some courses may have prerequisites or recommended prior knowledge, so be sure to check the course details before enrolling.


    • Complete Assignments: As you progress through the course, you'll encounter assignments designed to assess your understanding and application of animation concepts. These assignments are automatically graded using our machine learning model. Submit your completed assignments within the specified deadlines to receive timely feedback and marks.


      Utilize the Animation Creation Tool: To enhance your learning experience and practice animation principles, our platform provides an animation creation tool. Access the tool within the course materials or through a dedicated section of the platform. Familiarize yourself with the tool's interface and functionalities.

    • Learn and Practice: Follow the course materials, which may include video lectures, reading materials, and interactive exercises. Engage actively with the content and utilize the animation creation tool to create simple 2D animations. Apply the animation principles taught in the course and experiment with different techniques to solidify your understanding.


      Seek Mentorship and Collaboration: Take advantage of the interactive features of our platform. Engage with established animation designers, industry professionals, and other learners through forums, discussion boards, or virtual meetups. Seek mentorship, ask questions, share your work, and collaborate on projects with your peers.


    • Track Progress and Achievements: Monitor your progress within the course and track your achievements. Our platform provides a dashboard or progress tracker that displays your completed courses, grades, and any earned certifications or badges.


      Provide Feedback: We value your feedback to continuously improve our platform. Feel free to share your thoughts, suggestions, or any issues you encounter through our feedback system or by contacting our support team.
